The eye behind

OUR STORY

Born from a passion for light and shadow

Noir & Ivory Imaging was founded on a simple belief: photography is not about capturing what is — it's about revealing what could be. We see the world in contrasts, in the interplay between darkness and light, between silence and movement.

Our dual focus on event and real estate photography stems from a fascination with two fundamental aspects of human experience — the moments we share and the spaces we inhabit.

Based in Los Angeles, we bring a cinematic sensibility to every shoot. By stripping away color, we reveal the essential architecture of each moment, the raw geometry of emotion and space.

THE PROCESS

01

Consultation

We begin with a detailed conversation to understand your vision, the space, and the story you want to tell.

02

The Capture

On the day, we work with precision and intuition — reading light, anticipating moments, composing each frame with intention.

03

The Develop

Every image is meticulously processed in our digital darkroom, crafting the final visual narrative with care and artistry.
